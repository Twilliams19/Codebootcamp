History

Taylor Williams was born in California, but spent much of his life in Georgia. He always thought of Georgia as home. He has many friends and family that reside in Georgia. He is the son of Cynthia and Harold Smith. His maternal family is originally from Laurel, Delaware, where many continue to reside, including his only living grandparent. Taylor has always enjoyed playing video games, whether it was Xbox games or a PC game. This is what sparked his intrest in Coding. This lead to his life goal to learn Graphic Design and Coding. Taylor received his early education in private schools including Romar Acadamy, Atlanta New Century School, The Cottage School and Brandon Hall. Smaller class sizes were critical to his educational success. All of these schools are located in the Atlanta area. In 2010, he graduated high school at Brandon Hall. During his college years, Taylor focused on getting a degree in Criminal Justice. During college he worked a part-time job at Domino's Pizza and later worked at United Parcel Service(UPS). In 2015, Taylor received his Associates Degree from Georgia Perimeter College. In 2015, he entered Georgia State University to get his Bachelor's in Criminal Justice. In his senior year, he completed an internship at Georgia Bureau of Investigation(GBI), in the Financial Investigation Unit. Later, in 2018, he graduated Georgia State University(GSU) with his degree. He is currently work part-time for a security company and focusing on gaining admission to coding school. He hopes to use his knowledge in coding and his degree in Criminal Justice to gain employment in a government agency or Fortune 500 company.
